Famous Places — Definitions (Definition List)

Charminar
An iconic 16th-century mosque and monument in Hyderabad.
Golconda Fort
A historic fort known for its unique acoustic system.
Konark Sun Temple
A 13th-century temple dedicated to the Sun God, UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Chilika Lake
Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, famous for migratory birds.
