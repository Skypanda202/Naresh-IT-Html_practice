Headings

Headings are used to represent important text in HTML pages. There are 6 heading tags: h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, and h6.
They decrease in size and importance from h1 to h6.
All headings are bold by default.

Anchor Tag

The <a> tag is used to create links to other pages, files, or actions.
The href attribute contains the destination URL or path.
To open in a new tab, use target="_blank".

1. Simple link to website: Go to Google
<a href="https://www.google.com">Go to Google</a>

2. Open link in new tab: Open Netflix in new tab
<a href="https://www.netflix.com" target="_blank">Open Netflix in new tab</a>
